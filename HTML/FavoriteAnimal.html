Canis Dirus

The Dire Wolf

The dire wolf is an extinct species of the genus Canis.

Dire Wolf Vs. Saber Tooth Cat

The dire wolf is one of the most famous prehistoric carnivores in North America, along with its extinct competitor, the sabre-toothed cat Smilodon fatalis.

Size of a Dire Wolf

The Dire Wolf stood just over 2 feet tall (between 25 to 30 inches) and weighed on average 110 pounds with a maximum weight of 150 pounds.
