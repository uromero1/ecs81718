Jesse Jackson

Jesse Louis Jackson Sr.

Jesse Jackson is an american civil rights activist, Baptist minister and politician.
He was a canidate for the democratic presidential nomination in 1984 and 1988.
He also served as a shadow U.S Senator for the District of Columbia from 1991 to 1997.
